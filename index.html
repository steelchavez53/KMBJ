KMBJ GENERAL CONTRACTOR INC, residential and commercial building construction company in California.

Company licensed by The Contractors State License Board to carry out general contractor work in the construction of residential and commercial buildings, from earthworks to equipment and commissioning. With headquarters in Los Angeles, California. It has 19 years of experience in the construction industry and meets quality, occupational safety, and insurance requirements.

What We Do

We manage and construct residential and commercial buildings from demolition, earthmoving, civil works, trenching excavation, structure construction, electrical network installation, plumbing, mechanical works, architectural finishes, landscaping, equipment, and commissioning until handover in operation.
