About KMBJ General Contractor

History and experience

KMBJ General Contractor started as a subcontractor specializing in structural steel in August 2005. In 2024, it began operations as a General Contractor with all the experience and assets previously gained in the construction industry, focusing on the luxury residential construction sector in the city of Los Angeles and surrounding areas.

  • Our Mission
  • Our Values

KMBJ GENERAL CONTRACTOR INC is a company dedicated to the construction of luxury residential and commercial buildings, focusing on finishes and details. In accordance with the "B- General Building Contractor" license granted by the Contractors State License Board (https://www.cslb.ca.gov/) , we handle projects from foundation to finishing, serving the Los Angeles area and Southern California. Our office is located at 12929 Telegraph Rd Unit I, Santa Fe Springs, California, CA 90670. With 19 years of experience in the building industry, initially as subcontractors and now as General Contractor, we possess the necessary safety licenses, as well as financial and legal guarantees for our work
